The problem

Most industrial startups don't fail because the technology doesn't work.

They fail because it doesn't hold up in real-world conditions.

  • Wrong use case
  • Wrong assumptions
  • No path to adoption
What we offer

We combine capital with early real-world insight.

  • Access to construction companies as early input providers
  • Feedback on real operational constraints
  • Earlier validation of use cases
  • Better understanding of where your solution actually fits
What we look for

We back a small number of teams.

This is a fit if...
  • Clear relevance to a real industrial problem
  • Technical depth - not surface-level innovation
  • Teams that can operate in industrial environments (long cycles, constraints, complexity)
This is not a fit if...
  • You are still exploring use cases
  • You need fast iteration cycles
  • Your solution depends on ideal conditions
