Technology is not the problem. Adoption is.

The European construction industry doesn't lack ideas. It lacks early alignment around what actually gets built and adopted.

In a fragmented value chain, no single company is large enough to shape how new technologies are developed. So most wait - and try to adopt later.

Startups move in isolation, building for a market they don't fully understand. By the time both find each other, it is too late.

Not because the technology fails — but because it doesn't survive the reality of a job site.

What's missing isn't more innovation. It's early commitment - coordinated across the companies that will actually use it.

This is why we are here.

We don't just fund technology, we fund early integration.

Who we are

Founded by Felix, INPUT Partners is a deal-by-deal investment syndicate.

The principle: Long-term investors invest alongside SMEs from the industry who don't just provide capital - they put "skin in the game." They become design partners to validate market demand and actively help shape integration. This enables structured reduction of market risk while the technology is still being developed.
