Technology is not the problem. Adoption is.

The European construction industry doesn't lack technological innovation. It lacks early integration with the operational realities of the customers that use them.

Within the fragmented value chain most companies are too small to shape how new technologies are developed. So they wait - and try to adopt later.

Startups move in isolation, building for a market they don't fully understand. By the time both find each other, it is too late.

Not because the technology fails — but because it doesn't survive the reality of a job site.

What's missing isn't more innovation. It's early commitment - shared among the companies that will actually use it.

This is why we are here.

Who we are

INPUT Partners is a deal-by-deal investment syndicate focused on industrial adoption.

We bring together long-term investors and construction SMEs. Not just to fund technology, but to commit early and shape what gets built. Our members don't observe innovation. They participate in it, with capital, real-world input, and early integration.
